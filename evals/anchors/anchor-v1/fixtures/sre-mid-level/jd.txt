Senior Site Reliability Engineer
Lattice Cloud — Remote (US)

About the role
Lattice Cloud runs a multi-region container platform serving 1,200+ enterprise customers. The infrastructure team is hiring an SRE to own the reliability of our control plane: ingress, scheduling, and the metadata layer. You'll set SLOs, drive postmortem culture, and reduce on-call burden through deliberate engineering investment.

What you'll do
- Own incident response for the control plane: lead postmortems, identify systemic patterns, drive corrective actions to completion.
- Define and instrument SLOs and SLIs for our scheduling pipeline; partner with product engineering to fold reliability budgets into roadmap planning.
- Improve observability: extend our Prometheus/Grafana stack, add structured tracing where it's missing, write runbooks that cut MTTR.
- Author Terraform modules that codify infrastructure standards across teams; review IaC changes from product engineers.
- Participate in a 24/7 on-call rotation (one week in five). Mentor junior engineers on incident command.
- Run blameless postmortems and own the action-item tracker.

What we're looking for
- 4+ years operating production distributed systems at scale (preferably Kubernetes-based).
- Strong programming in at least one of: Python, Go.
- Hands-on experience with: Prometheus/Grafana, Terraform, AWS or GCP, structured logging, distributed tracing.
- Track record of leading incident response and writing postmortems other engineers actually read.
- Comfort with on-call as a craft, not a chore.

Nice to have
- Service mesh experience (Istio, Linkerd).
- Previous SRE-track promotion or staff-level scope.
- Open-source contributions to observability tools.

Compensation
$185–$225K base + equity. Full benefits, $2K/yr learning budget, fully remote with quarterly team gatherings.
