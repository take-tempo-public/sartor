Senior Product Manager, Provider Workflows
Atrium Health Platform — Hybrid, Boston

About Atrium
Atrium Health Platform sells revenue-cycle automation to mid-size hospital systems. Our product reduces denied-claim rates and shortens billing cycles for our customers. We are post-Series-C, profitable, growing the platform team to take on a new EHR-integration surface in 2026.

The role
You'll own the Provider Workflows product area — the part of our platform that clinical teams interact with daily. The current PM is rotating to a new initiative; this hire takes the existing roadmap, ships the next two quarters, and begins discovery on the EHR integration line.

Responsibilities
- Drive product strategy and roadmap for Provider Workflows, balancing hospital-system stakeholder needs against engineering capacity.
- Run discovery: customer interviews, usage analytics, qualitative research with clinical staff. Synthesize findings into prioritized opportunity briefs.
- Partner with design and engineering through the full delivery lifecycle: scoping, build, launch, instrumentation, post-launch iteration.
- Own KPIs for the area: feature adoption, denial-rate reduction, time-to-resolution. Defend tradeoffs in quarterly business reviews.
- Mentor an associate PM on prioritization and customer-discovery craft.

What we're looking for
- 7+ years product management in B2B SaaS, ideally with workflow products used by domain experts (clinicians, lawyers, finance ops).
- Demonstrated discovery skill — you have customer-interview habits, not just opinions.
- Strong written communication; you write opportunity briefs that stakeholders read end-to-end.
- Comfort presenting to executive customers and to your own exec team.
- Cross-functional leadership without authority — design, eng, sales, support all reporting elsewhere.

Nice to have
- Healthcare or healthtech product background.
- Familiarity with EHR systems (Epic, Cerner) or HL7/FHIR data models.
- Experience with revenue-cycle workflows or claims processing.

Compensation
$210–$260K base + equity. Hybrid Boston (3 days/week in office).
