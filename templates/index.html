Career corpus
Your career corpus is the pool of experiences the LLM draws from when composing a résumé. Build it by importing a résumé (AI extracts your experience automatically) or by adding experiences and bullets by hand — both work, and you can mix them. Each experience holds one or more titles (one official, optional alternates) plus its bullets. Edits here apply to the next application, not any in-flight one.
