# Candidate memory — what sartor remembers

> **Purpose:** the user-facing explanation of candidate memory — what it stores and
> why it matters.
> **Audience:** `user` — no technical background assumed.
> **Grounding:** the Candidate memory tab in `templates/index.html` (`#panelMemory`);
> mirrors the in-app memory help.

---

**Candidate memory** keeps the questions sartor asked you during **Clarify** and
the answers you gave, across **every** application. It's a running record of the
real details you've surfaced about your work.

## Why it's useful
Answers with **concrete numbers and outcomes** make the strongest new résumé
bullets, so sartor highlights those — they're the best raw material for tailoring
future résumés (see [[tailoring-a-resume]]) and for promoting into your
[[career-corpus]]. Nothing here is shared between users.

See [[using-sartor]] for how clarifying questions fit into the first run.
